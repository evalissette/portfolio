· A bit about me ·

B.Eng in Mechatronics Engineering from Chungnam National University in South Korea, currently MSc in User Centered Interactive Systems from Veracruzana University in Mexico, passionate about the UX research and design.

Experience with programming languages such as C and Python, also possess domain over HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I've worked on IDEs as Arduino, Jupyter Notebook and Visual Studio.

Proficient over the UX/UI design process, tools, methodologies and heuristics, with over 2 years of experience using Figma for UI design.

Academic background

During my B.Eng I became acquainted with programming and with the process of product design. Therefore became more interested in how a digital product could solve problems or necessities of a specific population.

UX Research and UI Design

The UX research and design has been key to answer my posed inquiry, and also it let me be aware of how important it is to study and deepen into the context and characteristics of the population of interest before the development of a digital product. A goo UX research will always give a positive impact to the UI Design.

Interests & Abilities

I am a languages enthusiast, and also a psychology student, which complements the study of the populations of interest while working in the UX research and artifacts design. As well I possess a deep interest in art, philosophy and scientific research.
